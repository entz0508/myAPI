Welcome to Dora’s Learning Adventure!

Hello, thank you for joining Dora and friends on their exciting adventures.
Dora’s Learning Adventure is a mobile and truly multi-sensory edutainment service
that delivers age-targeted episodes for smartphones and tablets, enabling young
children to explore and self-learn at their own pace through a personal and highly
interactive fun experience with their beloved and trusted friend, Dora the Explorer.


Dora the Explorer is a trusted children’s brand of Nickelodeon based on a certified
curriculum designed by educational and cultural experts that is distributed in 151
markets in 30 languages with over 200 million people watching and learning from
Dora every year. Nickelodeon’s programming and products are known to promote
social and cognitive development by teaching thinking skills in a playful way.
The content is safe, reliable, innovative and original.


Dora’s Learning Adventure is your child’s personal digital friend for playful,
learning adventures anytime, anywhere. Kids have never been so personally entertained
through playful digital learning with Dora and her friends!
We hope your adventure with Dora is full of fun surprises!
